ClimbFit Support

About

ClimbFit launched for iOS on November 14, 2016.

It is designed as a simple and inuitive way to track your Rockclimbing workouts. You can track grades and number of moves for each route or boulder problem.

Privacy Policy

  1. ClimbFit does not sync or upload any of the data you enter into the ClimbFit application
  2. ClimbFit application does not access any information on your device, residing outside of the ClimbFit application.
  3. You can export your data at anytime from within the application, via a CSV file.

Updates

An updated version of ClimbFit is underway. It is expected in early December 2016. It will include updates to the following:

  • Updated and new Charts
  • Ability to add System and Campus Board workouts
