About

SoundBlocks launched for iOS on April 10, 2017.

Imagine a recording studio built out of brightly colored children's blocks. Sound and music are blocks of color. Alter the sounds by stretching the block. Drag the blocks into different configurations, build a soundscape out of colors. This is the concept behind SoundBlocks. A simple and intuitive way to record, manipulate and combine audio clips.

Recording sounds is simple, hold down the record button and when you are finished the clip is shown as a colored block. Stretch or squeeze the blocks to change their pitch. Compress or expand them to distort them in different ways. You can combine blocks into sequences by dragging the blocks into the tracks at the bottom of the screen.

Privacy Policy

  1. SoundBlocks does not sync or upload any of the audio you record or create in the SoundBlocks application
  2. SoundBlocks does not access any information on your device, residing outside of the SoundBlocks application.
  3. You can export your audio at anytime from within the application, after purchasing a non-consumeable In-App-Purchase.
