ClimbFit Version 1.1

December 14, 2016

ClimbFit v1.1 is now available in the Apple App Store.

This version consists mostly of migrating the code base to Swift 3, but also includes the ability to change grading systems to Australian or French systems. Version 1.2 should be out in January and include more functionality.

ClimbFit Launch

November 12, 2016

On November 10, 2016, ClimbFit launched in the Apple App Store.

ClimbFit is designed to be a simple and intuitive way to track your rockclimbing workouts. You can track grade and number of moves for each route or boulder problem. Future updates will include the ability to track Campus and System board sessions.
